About Me

Hey there! I’m Grabster, A 22 year old digital artist, content creator and tech enthusiast from Pakistan. I invest most of my time making art and watching molded memes on YouTube. That fart sound man. I can't get it out of my head.

I've been making YouTube videos since 2015 and have built an amazing community of over 18,000 people. My goal is to teach new skills with easy to follow instructions, beautiful visuals and fun commentary.

For the past couple years I have been interested in Android customization and theming in general. I have designed numerous presets for Kustom Live Wallpaper Maker (also known as KLWP) and I also made my own Icon Packs Reev Pro, Reev Dark and an open source weather icon pack called Weather Now.

When I’m not working, I listen to music and play games. I'm told my music taste is quite sh*t, but that's just me I guess. I’m mostly interested in heavy bass (dubstep/metal) and synthwave. My gaming taste however is very specific. I only play fast paced games that keeps me on the edge such as the DOOM franchise, DiRT and old-school Contra games.

I’m mostly active on Twitter and Discord. I do exist on other social media sites, but I rarely use them. If you want to find me, my username is usually always @GrabsterTV across all platforms.
