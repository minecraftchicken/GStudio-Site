Evolution for Kustom

Evolution is a Komponent pack for Kustom apps (KLWP, KWGT and KLCK) that brings old Android aesthetic all the way from Android Sooner (the first ever version of Android) to Android 12..

What are Komponents?

"Komponent" is a reusable layer or item in Kustom apps that contains globals and other items such as text, shapes, formulas etc. Think of a Komponent as a preset within a preset. You can import a Komponent into your existing preset to integrate it into your theme seamlessly and be able to edit it non-destructively.

How to import a Komponent?

  1. Open the Kustom app you want to import the Komponent into. Same steps apply across all Kustom apps.
  2. In the editor, tap the + icon at the top right corner to create a new item.
  3. Select Komponent.
  4. Find and select Komponent you want to use.
  5. The Komponent will now be added into your preset and you can edit it like any other item.

How to edit a Komponent?

Some developers choose not to make their Komponent fully editable. Meaning with some Komponents like Evolution, the Komponent is edit locked. You will not be able to edit individual items inside the Komponent. You can only edit what the developer has made available to you through GLOBALS.
To see those globals, open the GLOBALS tab inside the Komponent and explore what is available to you. The GLOBALS tab will be your default view if the Komponent is locked.
