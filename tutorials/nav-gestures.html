Navigation gestures

Google has made it difficult to use navigation gestures with third party launchers since Android 10. While some manufacturers made it possible to use them, others simply refuse to work. This article will list all workarounds you need to know across (almost) all Android skins to make gestures work with your preferred launcher.

Samsung OneUI:

Samsung allows gestures with third party launchers, but it has a stupid delay when opening recent apps from the home screen. Here's a workaround to eliminate it...

  1. Install your preferred GoodLock launcher. I prefer FineLock
  2. Open it and navigate to Left Handle.
  3. Go to Diagonal Down or Up, whichever you prefer and select Recent Key from the list.
  4. Repeat the process if you want the same gesture to apply on the right side as well.
  5. On your home screen, make the gesture you set recent key to and it will immediately open your recent apps screen without delay.

Limitation: Does not work with OneUI Core due to lack of GoodLock support.

Xiaomi MIUI:

If you have a Xiaomi phone, you're out of luck. They've made use of any third party gesture app increasingly difficult to set up. Until a workaround is in sight, the best you can do right now, is not use a third party launcher.
