Creating Home Pages

Press the home button on your phone and scroll horizontally. This is where you put your widgets and icons. Each time you scroll, you're switching pages. These are called home pages or home screens.

Why?

Empty home pages are essential for KLWP presets that function as launchers by themselves and requires the home pages to be empty, so they are not obscured by widgets or icons.

What you need:

This tutorial will only cover Nova Launcher and Lawnchair. If you're using a pre-installed launcher, you may be out of luck because chances are, it does not support empty pages or wallpaper scroll which are both required for KLWP. To proceed, make sure you have either Nova launcher or Lawnchair installed.

In Nova Launcher:

  1. Tap and hold on your home screen to bring up the home options.
  2. Swipe to the left multiple times until you see a home page with + icon.
  3. Tap it to create a new empty home page.
  4. Tap "Create blank page" when asked.

In Lawnchair v12+:

Lawnchair currently does not let you create empty home pages. However, there is an easy workaround to make it working.

  1. Install Blank Icon/Widget by Straight Up.
  2. In Lawnchair, tap and hold to bring up the home screen options.
  3. Tap widget to open widgets list.
  4. Search for Blank Icon and expand it.
  5. Tap and hold on the Blank Widget and take it to the right edge of the screen. Place it on this new home page somewhere you won't be placing any icons or widgets at. My preferred location is on the top left of the sccreen or in the middle.
  6. If you created a widget for the first time in Lawnchair, you will be prompted to allow widget creation. Check Allow Lawnchair to create widgets and access their data and then tap Create
