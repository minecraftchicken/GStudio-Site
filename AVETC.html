A Very Epic Theme Contest

Abbreviated "AVETC", is a monthly Android theming contest where participants are given a set of rules to follow and make a theme. 3 of the best entries, voted by themers like you, win codes to apps from various developers including myself.

General Contest Rules

  1. Only 1 theme per user is allowed.
  2. Each month's contest is unique and has a specific set of rules. You must follow them. These rules can be found below.
  3. Theme must be your own creation. Widget presets can be used, but try making your own to stand out from the crowd.
  4. No collaboration or asking for feedback is allowed.
  5. Submitting framed screenshots are allowed but there must be 1 additional screenshot without any frames, so your theme can be better viewed.
  6. Entry must be tweeted with hashtag #AVETC06. The number at the end represents the running contest month.THIS IS VERY IMPORTANT. If you get this wrong, I won't be able to find your entry.

Rules For AVETC06

  1. This month, make use of the 3 empty rectangles and the empty space around them. No space limitations. Be creative and make it beautiful!
  2. Keep the aesthetic minimal. Try not to use loud fonts or imagery.
  3. You can only use wallpapers from this collection, but as always, it contains PNG overlays so you can use your own wallpaper if you choose to.

    4. Feeling lost? No worries, Click here to see an example theme.

Winners & Prizes

  1. Contest will end March 5th, 2022.
  2. 6 submissions will be selected based on its creativity, uniqueness and accessability.
  3. UPDATED A poll will be created that will run for 48 hours. During this time, other themers will vote for their preferred theme.
  4. At the end of 48 hours, top 3 most voted entries will win the contest.
  5. Winners must DM me within the next 48 hours of poll results to claim their prizes.

Prizes for AVETC06

  1. Fluid Walls — Stark Designs
  2. Reev Pro — Grabster Studios

    3. Sponsor this contest by giving away codes. Get in touch via Twitter DM.

In A Very Epic Theme Contest 6 - #AVETC06, you have full creative freedom, again.

Run your creativity wild and create a beautiful, minimalist home screen!
