A Very Epic Theme Contest

Abbreviated "AVETC", is a monthly Android theming contest where participants are given a set of rules to follow and make a theme. 3 of the best entries, voted by themers like you, win codes to apps from various developers including myself.

General Contest Rules

  1. Only 1 theme per user is allowed.
  2. Each month's contest is unique and has a specific set of rules. You must follow them. These rules can be found below.
  3. Theme must be your own creation. You can use presets or widgets.
  4. No collaboration or asking for feedback is allowed.
  5. Submitting framed screenshots are allowed but there must be 1 additional screenshot without any frames, so your theme can be better viewed.
  6. Entry must be tweeted with hashtag #AVETC04. The number at the end represents the running contest month.THIS IS VERY IMPORTANT. If you get this wrong, I won't be able to find your entry.

Rules For AVETC04

  1. Let's give some love to our Docks. Make a unique and beautiful dock that no one has ever seen!
  2. Use wallpapers from this Unsplash collection only: A Space for Dock.
  3. Though not against the rules, try not only put icons in the dock. Be creative with it. Maybe show something useful alongside icons.

Winners & Prizes

  1. Contest will end January 5, 2022.
  2. 6 submissions will be selected based on the theme's creativity, uniqueness and general design.
  3. A poll will be created that runs for 48 hours where the general public chooses which of the 6 submissions they like most.
  4. At the end of 48 hours, top 3 most voted entries will win the contest.
  5. Winners must DM me within the next 48 hours of the poll to claim their prizes.

Prizes for AVETC04

  1. Tessa Icon Pack — Icarus
  2. Prima Kustom Widgets — Icarus
  3. Vera Icon Pack — One4Studio
  4. MiniWall Wallpapers — ArrowWalls

    5. Sponsor this contest by giving away codes. Get in touch via Twitter DM.

In A Very Epic Theme Contest 03 - #AVETC04, you have full creative freedom... EXCEPT the font style.

You are required to only use a Monospace font from Google fonts as your primary font. The rest is up to you.
